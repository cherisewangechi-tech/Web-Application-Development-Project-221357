About our Circuit

Our Mission and Vision

Founded with the goal of developing elite golf talent, the Kenya Amateur Golf Circuit provides a highly competitive environment for serious amateur golfers to hone their skills.

Our tour organizes multi-round stroke play championships across the country's premier courses, offering a professional tournament experience for low-handicap players.

Tournament Eligibility

Our events are open to male and female amateur golfers holding a valid club handicap index. We run strictly regulated events to ensure fair, challenging, and authentic competitive conditions that conform to international R&A rules.
