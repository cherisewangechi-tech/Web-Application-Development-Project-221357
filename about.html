About the Circuit

Governing elite competitive amateur golf, fostering sportsmanship, and managing the official seasonal Order of Merit metrics.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded with a commitment to high-level competition, the National Amateur Golf Circuit serves as the premier proving ground for top-tier amateur golfers. The tour provides a highly structured environment where players can test their skills across demanding course layouts under strict international regulations.

Through multi-round stroke play championships and collaborative regional opens, we seek to elevate the standards of competitive sportsmanship while maintaining an authoritative national ranking registry that directly impacts seasonal selections.

Elite Standard

Strict registration entry fields limited to verified low-handicap competitors.

Order of Merit

Unified, point-based ranking tracking performance consistency across all tour stops.
