About the Circuit

ELEVATING AMATEUR GOLF EXCELLENCE

Founded with a vision to streamline competitive pathways, the Kenya Amateur Golf Circuit serves as the premier tour framework for regional talent. We bridge the gap between casual club play and elite national tracking, offering players standardized scoring matrices, immaculate venue selections, and transparent event management.

By centering our platform around authentic amateur status fields, we cultivate an environment where strategy, focus, and true sporting character take center stage. Through our partnerships with top-tier hosting clubs, every seasonal fixture delivers a true championship testing ground.
