Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who is eligible to participate in the circuit events?

A: The circuit is strictly open to male and female golfers holding valid amateur status with an active handicap index verifiable via their official club handicap system.

Q: How are seasonal Order of Merit points calculated?

A: Points are awarded based on final position finishes in each sanctioned event. Your top finishes accumulate over the season to determine the final championship standings.

Q: What is the cancellation policy for tournament entries?

A: Players must withdraw at least 48 hours prior to the official day-one tee time to receive a full entry fee credit for subsequent calendar fixtures.
