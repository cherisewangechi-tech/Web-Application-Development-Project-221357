Frequently Asked Questions

A: The circuit is strictly open to male and female golfers holding valid amateur status with an active handicap index verifiable via their official club handicap system.

A: Points are awarded based on final position finishes in each sanctioned event. Your top finishes accumulate over the season to determine the final championship standings.

A: All events are strictly governed by the Rules of Golf as approved by the R&A Rules Limited, alongside specific Local Rules enacted by individual host venue tournament committees.

A: Registrations can be submitted directly through our online registration portal or via the tournament secretariat desk at the designated host club before the entry deadline.

A: Ties for gross winner positions are resolved via a sudden-death hole playoff immediately following conclusion of play, while count-backs are utilized for net positions based on scorecard layouts.

A: Yes, players may use devices that measure distance only. Devices that measure slope, wind speed, or temperature are strictly prohibited under tournament play rules.

A: Official draw sheets are finalized and uploaded to the tour portal 48 hours prior to the first round of competition, and are also displayed on host club notices.

A: Cancellations made more than 72 hours prior to the scheduled first round tee time are eligible for full credit towards the next circuit event. Late withdrawals are non-refundable.
