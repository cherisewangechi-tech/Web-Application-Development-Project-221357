General Enquiries

How do I register for an upcoming event?

Navigate to our Register page, fill out your handicap index details, and submit the entry form before the close of registration on the Tuesday preceding the tournament.

Are caddies permitted during tournament rounds?

Yes, caddies are fully permitted. However, players are strictly responsible for their caddie's conduct and adherence to local club regulations throughout the round.

What happens in the event of a tie?

Ties for first place will be decided by a sudden-death playoff on a hole designated by the tournament committee. All other ties split seasonal points evenly.
