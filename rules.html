Tournament Governance

Official Local Rules

🚩

Section A: General Play & Bunker Exceptions

All circuit events are governed strictly under the R&A Rules of Golf alongside designated local club mandates. Any ball resting within an unraked footprint or abnormal hazard context must be treated strictly according to committee stipulations regarding relief.

⏱️

Section B: Strict Pace of Play Policies

To maintain circuit flow, marshal groups track group timings. A group is considered out of position if it falls more than 14 minutes behind the preceding field flight. Review timing metrics to prevent immediate stroke penalties.
