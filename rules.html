Rules & Regulations

Circuit Local Rules

All tournaments under the Kenya Amateur Golf Circuit are played in strict accordance with the Rules of Golf as approved by the R&A, alongside the following local regulations:

1. Handicap Index Requirements

Players must present an active, verified club handicap index upon registration. The maximum allowed handicap index for entry into the premier division is 6.0.

2. Pace of Play Policy

A group is deemed out of position if they fall more than one clear hole behind the group ahead. First offense results in a formal warning; subsequent offenses incur a one-stroke penalty.

3. Distance Measuring Devices

A player may obtain distance information by using a device that measures distance only. Devices that measure slope, wind speed, or temperature are strictly prohibited during tournament rounds.
